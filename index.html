FAQ

What areas in Calgary do you deliver to?

We deliver across Calgary and nearby communities. Share your postal code on WhatsApp to confirm availability.

Do you offer same-day delivery or catering?

Same-day pickup is sometimes available for select dishes. Catering requests need 48-72 hours notice.

What are your delivery times and business hours?

Delivery windows are typically late morning through evening, Monday to Saturday. Exact times are confirmed per order.

Can you accommodate food allergies and dietary restrictions?

Yes. Please add detailed allergy notes in the order or booking form so we can confirm safe options.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely. We offer plant-based soups, rice dishes, and sides. Ask us for a tailored vegetarian menu.

Can I customize dishes or make special requests?

Yes. Share spice level preferences, ingredient swaps, or special notes in the order form or WhatsApp.

How spicy are your dishes? Can I adjust spice levels?

We offer mild, medium, and hot options. Tell us your preference and we will tailor the spice.

What is the minimum order for catering services?

Small gathering packages start at $150. Custom quotes are available for 20+ guests.

Can you handle large events (50-100+ guests)?

Yes. We offer scalable catering trays, staffing support, and custom menus for large events.

Do you provide serving utensils and disposable plates?

Disposable plates and serving utensils are available upon request. Let us know your needs in advance.

How does WhatsApp ordering work?

Tap any WhatsApp button to send a pre-filled message, then we confirm details and send payment info.

Can I order through the website or must I use WhatsApp?

You can build your order on the website and submit it here, or use WhatsApp for the fastest response.

How will I receive my order confirmation?

We send confirmation by WhatsApp plus email and SMS where available, including pickup or delivery timing.

What payment methods do you accept?

eTransfer, credit/debit card, and cash on pickup are accepted. Deposits may be required for large events.

Do you require a deposit for catering orders?

For larger events, a deposit secures the date. We will confirm deposit terms after reviewing your request.
